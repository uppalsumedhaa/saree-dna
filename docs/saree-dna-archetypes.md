# Saree DNA — Archetypes

Working document. Nine archetypes, weave-anchored. The user will fill in each archetype's six dimensions; these descriptions feed the question-design (reverse-engineering) phase.

For each archetype, hit:

1. **Visual signal** — fabric, palette, drape
2. **Personality posture** — how she moves through the world
3. **Occasion fit** — where/when she wears it
4. **The "you are not"** — what this archetype rejects
5. **Anchor reference** — a person, character, designer who *is* her
6. **Sensory cue** — a smell, sound, room, weather, time of day that evokes her
